Project 2 - VIT GAME

The VIT University's experiences and activities are brought to life in an engrossing game style through the interactive Java Swing project known as VIT-Game. Players will be able to explore several facets of college life on the virtual VIT campus in this game, which seeks to provide them an immersive and fun gaming experience. The VIT campus's many buildings, including classrooms, libraries, labs, sports facilities, dormitories, and cafeterias, may be explored by players. Every locale provides various tasks and pursuits specific to that area. For instance, participants can complete tests or puzzles in classrooms, while they can unwind by playing virtual video games in athletic facilities.

Project 4 - VIT QUIZ

VIT-Quiz is an interactive quiz platform developed using Java Swing, a graphical user interface (GUI) toolkit for Java. The purpose of this project is to offer the Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT) community and other educational institutions a user-friendly and entertaining environment for quizzes. The VIT-Quiz application enables users to take part in a variety of quizzes on various themes or topics. Users may pick and launch quizzes, track their progress, and submit their responses thanks to the GUI's simple navigation. Using a list of options, users may choose the best answer in multiple-choice questions on the site.
